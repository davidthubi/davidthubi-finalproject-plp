Eye Care

At Tiba Healthcare Limited, we are committed to providing the best eye care in Kenya. Our team of highly skilled and experienced ophthalmologists, optometrists, and support staff are dedicated to ensuring that every patient receives the highest quality care.

Our eye care services include:

Comprehensive eye exams: We offer comprehensive eye exams to assess the health of your eyes and identify any potential issues. These exams may include visual acuity testing, refraction testing, and eye movements and coordination assessment.

Treatment for eye conditions: Our team is trained to diagnose and treat a wide range of eye conditions, including nearsightedness, farsightedness, astigmatism, cataracts, glaucoma, and age-related macular degeneration.

LASIK and other refractive surgery: If you are interested in vision correction surgery, we offer LASIK and other refractive surgery procedures to help you achieve your best possible vision.

Eye emergencies: If you are experiencing an eye emergency, such as a foreign object in the eye or sudden vision loss, we are equipped to provide timely and effective treatment.

Children's eye care: We offer specialized care for children's eye health, including regular eye exams and treatment for conditions such as amblyopia (lazy eye) and strabismus (crossed eyes).

Why Choose Tiba Healthcare Limited for Eye Care in Kenya?

Experienced team: Our team includes some of Kenya's most experienced and respected ophthalmologists and optometrists.

Comprehensive care: We offer a full range of eye care services, including comprehensive eye exams, treatment for eye conditions, LASIK and other refractive surgery, eye emergencies, and children's eye care.
