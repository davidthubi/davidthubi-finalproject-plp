Dental Care

At Tiba Healthcare Limited, we are committed to providing high-quality dental care to our patients. Our team of skilled and experienced dentists is dedicated to delivering compassionate and personalized care to all our patients. Whether you need a routine cleaning or more advanced treatment, we have the expertise and resources to meet your needs.

Here are some of the dental services we offer at Medicross Limited:

Cosmetic Dentistry: Our cosmetic dentistry services are designed to improve the appearance of our patient's teeth. These services include teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding.

Dental X-ray: Dental X-rays are diagnostic tests that use ionizing radiation to create detailed images of the inside of the teeth and surrounding bone. These images diagnose various dental conditions, including cavities, infections, and impacted teeth.

Dental Fillings: Dental fillings repair teeth damaged by cavities or trauma. Fillings are made of various materials, including composite resin, amalgam, and gold.

Dental Implants: Dental implants are artificial teeth that replace missing teeth. Implants are made of titanium and are surgically inserted into the jawbone. Once in place, they provide a secure and stable foundation for replacement teeth.

Fixed Braces: Fixed braces are an orthodontic treatment used to straighten and align the teeth. These braces are attached to the teeth and cannot be removed until treatment is complete.

Pediatric Braces: Paediatric braces are a type of orthodontic treatment that is specifically designed for children. These braces are used to straighten and align the teeth and may be fixed or removable, depending on the patient's needs.

Pediatric Dentistry: Paediatric dentistry is a specialty that focuses on the oral health of children. Our pediatric dentists are trained to care for children from infancy through adolescence and are skilled in diagnosing and treating various dental conditions.

Root Canal Treatment: Root canal treatment saves a tooth that is infected or damaged. During this procedure, the infected or damaged tooth tissue is removed, cleaned, and sealed.
