Doctor's Consultation

At Tiba Healthcare Limited Kenya, we offer a wide range of medical services, including primary care, specialist consultations, diagnostic tests, and surgical procedures. Our team of highly qualified and experienced doctors is dedicated to delivering compassionate and personalized care to every patient who visits our facilities.

Here are some of the consultation services we offer at Medicross Limited:

Chronic Disease Management: If you are living with a chronic medical condition, such as diabetes or hypertension, our doctors can help you manage your condition and achieve optimal health. We will work with you to develop a treatment plan that includes medication, lifestyle changes, and ongoing support to help you live a healthy and active life.

Minor Surgery: Our doctors are skilled in performing various minor surgical procedures, including removing skin lesions and foreign bodies. We use the latest technology and techniques to ensure the safety and success of these procedures.

Dental Care: Our team of dentists is dedicated to providing high-quality dental care to patients of all ages. We offer a wide range of services, including teeth cleanings, fillings, and extractions.

ENT Specialists: Our ear, nose, and throat (ENT) specialists are skilled in treating various conditions, including allergies, sinus issues, and hearing loss. We offer medical and surgical treatment options to help our patients achieve optimal health.

Physicians Clinics: Our physician's clinics offer comprehensive primary care for patients of all ages. Our doctors are trained to diagnose and treat various medical conditions and will work with you to develop a personalized treatment plan.

Nutritionist & Dietician: Our nutritionists and dieticians are trained to help patients optimize their health through healthy eating habits and lifestyle changes. We offer individualized nutrition plans to help our patients achieve their health goals.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology: Our team of obstetricians and gynecologists are dedicated to providing high-quality care for women throughout all stages of life. We offer prenatal care, delivery services, and menopause management, among other benefits

Eye Care: Our team of ophthalmologists is trained to diagnose and treat various eye conditions, including glaucoma, cataracts, and retinal disorders. We offer medical and surgical treatment options to help our patients maintain optimal eye health.

Telemedicine: We offer telemedicine consultations with our doctors for patients who cannot visit our facilities in person. This allows patients to receive medical care from the comfort of their own homes.
